# 2026 NF Data Workshop

**A satellite event of the 2026 Children's Tumor Foundation NF Conference**

Organized by the [Children's Tumor Foundation](https://www.ctf.org), [Sage Bionetworks](https://sagebionetworks.org), and [Pluto](https://pluto.bio)

---

Welcome to the NF Data Workshop! This hands-on session gives you the opportunity to explore real NF research datasets using three different tools and approaches. No matter your background — whether you've never written a line of code or you work with data regularly — there is a track designed for you.

## Choose Your Track

### Track 1 — Exploring NF Tumor Data with cBioPortal
> **Best for:** Everyone. No coding or accounts required.

Use the cBioPortal web interface to explore genomic and clinical data from NF tumors. Visualize mutations, copy number changes, and clinical correlations through an intuitive point-and-click interface.

[Go to Track 1 →](track-1-cbioportal/)

---

### Track 2 — Using AI to Analyze cNF Data
> **Best for:** Participants comfortable following step-by-step instructions. A Synapse account is required.

Apply AI-assisted tools to analyze cutaneous neurofibroma (cNF) datasets. Explore how machine learning approaches are being used to extract insights from NF research data.

[Go to Track 2 →](track-2-ai-cnf/)

---

### Track 3 — Analyzing NF Portal Datasets on Pluto
> **Best for:** Participants interested in interactive bioinformatics analysis. A Synapse account is required.

Use the Pluto platform to perform and visualize bioinformatic analyses on datasets from the NF Data Portal — no local software installation needed.

[Go to Track 3 →](track-3-pluto/)

---

## Before You Arrive

- **All tracks:** Bring a laptop with a modern web browser (Chrome or Firefox recommended).
- **Tracks 2 & 3:** You will need a free [Synapse account](https://www.synapse.org/register). Please register in advance — it can take a few minutes for your account to be approved.
- See [SETUP.md](SETUP.md) for full setup instructions.
