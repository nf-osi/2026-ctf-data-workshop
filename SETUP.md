# Setup Instructions

This page covers everything you need to do **before the workshop** to make sure you're ready to go on the day.

---

## All Tracks

**Bring a laptop** with a modern web browser installed. We recommend [Google Chrome](https://www.google.com/chrome/) or [Mozilla Firefox](https://www.mozilla.org/firefox/).

No software installation is required for any track.

---

## Tracks 2 & 3: Synapse Account

Tracks 2 and 3 use data hosted on the [NF Data Portal](https://nf.synapse.org), which is powered by Sage Bionetworks' Synapse platform. You will need a free Synapse account to access these datasets.

### Create a Synapse Account

1. Go to [synapse.org/register](https://www.synapse.org/register)
2. Fill in your name, email address, and a password
3. Check your email for a verification link and click it
4. Log in to confirm your account is active

> **Note:** Account approval is usually instant, but can occasionally take a few minutes. Please register before the day of the workshop.

### Request Access to Workshop Datasets

Some datasets may require you to certify your Synapse account or request specific access. Instructions for any additional access steps will be provided in the individual track instructions.

---

## Track 3: Pluto Account

Track 3 uses [Pluto Biosciences](https://pluto.bio) for interactive analysis. Workshop participants will be provided with access credentials or a workspace link on the day of the event. No advance setup is needed beyond having a Synapse account.

---

If you run into any issues, please reach out to the workshop organizers before the event.
